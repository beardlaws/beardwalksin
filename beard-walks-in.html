About

Beard Walks In is a video-first local discovery platform born out of the Beard Laws Network. The concept is simple: Beard walks into a local restaurant, bar, brewery, barbershop, or hidden gem — orders what looks good, talks to the people, and tells you exactly what he thinks.

No PR packages. No sponsored meals. No algorithm-chasing. Just an honest look at the places that make local communities worth living in.

Every stop on this site has been visited in person and reviewed on video. If it's got the Beard Approved badge, that means it's the real deal.

For Businesses

Getting featured on Beard Walks In means a real video review, a permanent listing on this site, and placement on the map.

1

Someone Submits Your Place

A customer, employee, or you submit via the Suggest a Stop form. Beard reads every submission.

2

Beard Walks In

No heads up. No special treatment. Beard walks in like any other customer and films the honest experience.

3

Video + Listing Go Live

Video publishes to the Beard Laws Network. Your business gets a permanent listing with your video, photos, ratings, and map pin.

4

Earn the Badge

If you earn it, Beard grants the official Beard Approved badge. Hang it in the window. It means something.
