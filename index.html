The Story

About
Beard Walks In

Beard Walks In started with a simple idea: there are incredible local spots hidden in small towns across America, and most people never find them because nobody's out there talking about them.

So Beard walks in. No reservations. No press passes. No advance notice. Just a regular guy who loves good food, good vibes, and good people — walking through the door like any other customer and filming what he finds.

Every review is honest. Every stop is paid for out of pocket. If a place is great, you'll know it. If it misses, Beard will tell you that too.

For Businesses

Getting featured on Beard Walks In means a real video review, a permanent listing, and placement on the map — no charge, no strings.

1

Someone Submits Your Place

A customer, employee, or you submit via the Suggest a Stop form. Beard reads every submission.

2

No heads up. No special treatment. Beard walks in like any other customer and films the honest experience.

3

Video + Listing Go Live

Video publishes to the Beard Laws Network. Your business gets a permanent listing with video, photos, Beard Score, and map pin.

4

Earn the Beard Was Here Stamp

If you earn it, Beard grants the official stamp. Hang it in the window. Put it on your website. It means something because it can't be bought.
